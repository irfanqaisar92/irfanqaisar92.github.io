Irfan Qaisar is a dedicated PhD student at Tsinghua University, specializing in building energy efficiency through innovative control strategies. His research focuses on occupant-centric control (OCC) strategies for HVAC systems, leveraging advanced machine learning techniques, including reinforcement learning, to optimize building energy use and indoor comfort. Irfan is proficient in building energy simulation, and programming for simulation and control, and has substantial experience with tools such as EnergyPlus, OpenStudio, and Modelica.
